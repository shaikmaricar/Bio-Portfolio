About

Twenty-five years, one long throughline.

I design, build, and lead the data and AI systems that businesses run on. That work has taken many shapes over the years — relational databases and HA clusters in the early 2000s, big-data and SharePoint farms in the 2010s, cloud lakehouses and MPP warehouses in the late 2010s, and most recently production-grade Agentic AI built on RAG, MCP, and multi-agent orchestration.

The throughline is the same in all of them: take a fuzzy, ambiguous business problem, decompose it into a system that actually works in production, and lead the people who build it. I spend my time somewhere between principal-level client conversations — KPIs, steering committees, current-vs-target state — and hands-on engineering with whatever stack the problem demands.

I'm a recognized authority across all four major cloud MPP platforms, a TOGAF and Zachman practitioner, a Kimball-certified DW lifecycle designer, and an active builder of LangGraph, AutoGen, CrewAI, and MCP-based agent systems. I still write the code.
